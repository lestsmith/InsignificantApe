An Insignificant Ape

One member of the species 'Homo Sapiens' in currently 7.6 billion members on this small blue dot where we all live. These are some of the things that either interest me or I have to get involved in as a father, husband, analyst developer amongst other roles ...

Topics

Ice Skating

I've been ice skating since I was 12 and played ice hockey very briefly. Here, where I live, ice skating is a seasonal sport taking place from October to March. I always get very keen during these winter months.

Inline Skating

During the summer months inline skating is possible. It's flat here and the weather is usually dry if not always warm.

Fudokan Karate

Fudokan karate is a style similar to shotokan karate, but with more emphasis on protecting one's self while executing those 10,000 hours of punches and block that are required to become proficient at a sport. The idea is that if your joints and muscles are not worn out then you can enjoy karate to a ripe old age.

Web Development

I've recently become (Once again) interested in web development as a hobby. I am a 20 year professional software developer working on ERP systems for the wholesale and now manufacturing industries. I enjoy a challenge and the paradigm shift to web development provides enough learning material for a long time to come.

Chess

My son is a successful chess player, I can't beat him. He plays for a local team and for his school and (Possibly most important) for fun. I wish I had the time to devote to learning the game to the level he has, but I'll have to be satisfied with being on the edges of this fine game.

Warhammer

My other son is developing an interest in Warhammer, specifically Age of Sigmar. Again I'm going to remain on the edges of this, but have to get some level of knowledge about the game. I also have to convert one room in the house to be a Warhammer room where building and painting the figures will take place as well as the table top games.

Aquariums & Tropical Fish

This is the newest of the hobbies and interests I've picked up as a father, we now have a 125 litre tank with a few fish in.

Work
